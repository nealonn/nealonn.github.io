About the Collection

This collection examines how similar facial expressions can convey humor or fear through different cinematic techniques. It includes iconic scenes from comedy and horror films, highlighting the power of facial expressions in storytelling.

By comparing scenes from different genres, the collection aims to shed light on the artistry behind acting and the emotional impact of films. It serves as a visual exploration of how actors use facial expressions to engage and move audiences.
